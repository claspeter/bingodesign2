6 How Draws Work

Draws are conducted live on the Bingo24-7 platform at the scheduled time. Each draw:

  • Uses a set of 90 numbered balls (1–90) drawn in a random sequence.
  • Follows standard 90-ball bingo rules: each card has 3 rows of 9 columns with 5 numbers per row (15 numbers total per card).
  • Awards a LINE prize to the first card to complete any single row.
  • Awards a FULL HOUSE (BINGO) prize to the first card to have all 15 numbers called.
  • Is drawn at the ball interval configured for that draw by the administrator.

Draws are conducted automatically by the platform system. Results are final once confirmed by the system. In the event of a technical failure during a draw, Bingo24-7 reserves the right to void and reschedule the draw, with all ticket costs refunded.
